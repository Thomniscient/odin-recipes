Vegan Fried Rice

Description

This is my absolute favorite vegan fried rice recipe! It has Indian and Asian flavors and can be cooked in under 20 minutes! The tofu packs this dish with protein and you can load a whole heap of veggies in as well! A perfect quick and easy meal that is sure to be a new addition in your rotation! This vegan fried rice also tastes amazing as leftovers, making a great option for meal prep!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 4 Green Onions
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Diced Carrot
  • 1 Sliced Bell Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Green Peas
  • 1/2 cup Corn
  • 1 tbsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 2 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 200 g Extra Firm Tofu, Crumbled
  • 150 g Silken Tofu
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Start by cooking your rice in a separate pot with water and salt. In the meantime start cooking the veggies in a separate pan.
  2. Saut√© the green onions & garlic in a small amount of oil for 30 seconds then add in the remaining veggies, spices & sesame oil & cook for 5 more minutes.
  3. Mix in the extra firm tofu and silken tofu until combined, then add in the cooked rice and mix again until everything is incorporated.
  4. Serve with green onions and sesame seeds.
