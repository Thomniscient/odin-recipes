Taco Pasta

Description

The pasta that is the best of both worlds, half taco and half pasta. Perfect for when you can’t decide which one to have and can be whipped up in under 20 minutes! This is a quick and easy meal to throw together after a long day of school or work. But although quick, this meal does not skimp on nutrients or flavor! It is packed with whole grains, fiber, veggies and plant protein!

Ingredients

  • 250 g Whole Wheat Pasta
  • 1 Onion
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • 1 cup Corn
  • 225 g Extra Firm Crumbled Tofu
  • 2 tbsp Taco Seasoning
  • 1.5 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 2.5-3 cups Salsa

Steps

  1. Sauté your onion for 3-4 minutes until translucent. Add in the garlic and bell pepper and sauté for another few minutes
  2. Add in the rest of the ingredients and cook until heated through.
  3. Add in your cooked pasta then top with avocado, green onions, cilantro, or jalapenos!
