Vegan Spinach and Artichoke Pasta

Description

This vegan spinach and artichoke pasta is creamy, cheesy, garlicy, and so easy to make! It is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner loaded with veggies and flavor! This creamy pasta sauce is made in a blender and is also dairy free, and vegan! If you love creamy pastas you are going to love this healthy spinach and artichoke pasta!

Ingredients

  • 225 g Whole Wheat Pasta
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1 can Artichoke Hearts
  • 200 g Frozen Spinach
  • 1 400g Package Silken Tofu
  • 1/2 cup Raw Cashews
  • 3/4 cup Plant Milk
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 3/4 cup Nutritional Yeast
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© your onion over medium heat until translucent then add in the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Next add in your chopped artichokes and spinach and cook for a few more minutes until heated through.
  2. While your veggies are cooking start making the sauce. Add the ingredients for the sauce into your blender and blend until completely smooth.
  3. Add your sauce to the spinach and artichoke mixture along with the cooked pasta and mix well. Cook until everything is well combined and heated through!
  4. Serve with some vegan parmesan or top with some additional nutritional yeast!
